Welcome to oWomaniya

Hi, we are organizing a hackathon (oWomaniya) for women in tech, where we will create a web/mobile app or a portal to help, educate and support women. This will be open source contribution.

Theme of hackathon is to address following issues:

  • Sexual harassment
  • Pregnancy discrimination
  • Appearance
  • Work-life imbalance
